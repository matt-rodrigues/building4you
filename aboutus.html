welcome


we are delighted to welcome you to our platform, dedicated to promoting inclusion and supporting the lgbtqapn+ community. as an ngo, we work tirelessly to create a safe space for those facing vulnerabilities, offering essential support.


at building4u, we believe health is a fundamental right. we provide vital healthcare services, including rapid testing for hiv and other stis, and specialized psychological support. our professionals ensure each individual feels valued and well cared for.


we also recognize the transformative power of art. our theater and painting classes offer opportunities for creativity, skill development, and personal expression, enhancing emotional well-being and a sense of belonging.


join us in this mission by becoming a volunteer or supporting us through financial donations. volunteers can help with classes, building maintenance, welcoming new people, and offering a listening ear. each contribution helps us expand our services and reach more people in need. thank you for your support!


thank you very much for your visit and support!
