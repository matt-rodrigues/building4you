welcome


we are delighted to welcome you to our platform, dedicated to promoting inclusion and providing essential support to the lgbtqapn+ community. we are an ngo that understands the importance of creating a safe and welcoming space for those facing vulnerabilities, and we work tirelessly to offer support in various areas.


at building4u, we believe that health is a fundamental right. therefore, we promote vital healthcare services, including rapid testing for hiv and other sexually transmitted infections (stis), as well as offering specialized psychological support. our team of professionals is prepared to provide the necessary assistance, ensuring that each individual feels valued and well cared for.


in addition to healthcare, we recognize the transformative power of art in people's lives. we offer theater and painting classes, creating opportunities for our participants to explore their creativity, develop new skills, and find a form of personal expression. these activities not only promote emotional well-being but also strengthen self-esteem and a sense of community belonging.


we invite you to join us in this mission. there are several ways to contribute to our project. if you want to make a difference, consider becoming a volunteer. your time and skills can have a significant impact on many lives. alternatively, you can support our work through financial donations. each contribution helps us expand our services and reach more people in need.


inclusion and support for the LGBTQAPN+ community are responsibilities for all of us. together, we can build a more just and welcoming future for everyone. follow our actions, participate in our activities, and help building4u continue transforming lives.


thank you very much for your visit and support!


the building4u team
