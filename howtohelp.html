how to help?


you can help our NGO in several meaningful ways. Your contribution is essential for us to continue providing vital support to the LGBTQAPN+ community.


one way to contribute is through financial donations. You can make a donation directly to our IBAN: IE29AIBK93115212345678. These donations enable us to expand our services and reach more people in need, ensuring that each individual receives the support they deserve


In addition to financial donations, you can also make a difference by dedicating your time and skills as a volunteer. We need volunteers to assist in various areas, such as:


Theater and painting classes: Help inspire and develop the creativity of our participants, providing them with a form of personal expression and strengthening their self-esteem.

Maintenance of building spaces: Collaborate in maintaining and improving our spaces, ensuring a safe and welcoming environment for everyone.

elcoming new people: Be a friendly and welcoming presence for those who come seeking help, helping them feel valued and welcome.

istening to those in need: Offer a friendly ear and attentive listening to those who need to vent and share their experiences.


Your support, whether through financial donations or volunteer work, is essential for us to continue our mission of inclusion and support for the LGBTQAPN+ community. Together, we can build a more just and welcoming future for everyone.
